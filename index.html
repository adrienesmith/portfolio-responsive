Adriene Smith

Greetings! My name is Adriene, and I'm a software engineering student at Thinkful. I currently live, study, work, and thrive in Seattle, WA.

I use HTML, CSS, and JavaScript daily at both school and at work. I'm having a blast learning how these languages interact to create and sustain the world around us.

Who Am I?

In my current role as a digital prepress technician, I develop workflows that automate file preparation for mass-marketing print production. I've worked on my current team for 5 years, and enjoy collaborating on the development of programs that streamline the prepress process.

In January 2021, I embarked on a journey to take the knowledge I've learned in my current career, add in an education at Thinkful, and move forward into the tech industry. Development services that I am working towards learning include:

  • Front-End Services:
    • Content creation
    • Design and customization
    • Functionality and interactivity
  • Back-End Services:
    • API integration
    • Database management
    • Security settings

When I'm off the clock, there's a good chance you'll find me on a hiking trail. I've lived in the Pacific Northwest for my entire life, and I love spending time in the beautiful wilderness that surrounds the city I call home.
